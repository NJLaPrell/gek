Rules

Create a list of rules to define how you want to decide which videos are sorted to which playlists.

Sort

New videos are sorted automatically every hour (configurable in your preferences). You can sort new videos any time by running the sort process.

Review

Any video that does not match any of your rules is placed in the unsorted list, where you can review the videos and manually sort them into a playlist or remove them from your unsorted list.

Watch

Your playlists have been curated! Use your playlist panel on the left to start watching!
